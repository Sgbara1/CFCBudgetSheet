The Center for Financial Capability (CFC), as a branch of the University of New Mexico Dean of Students Office, is a program dedicated to financial outreach and education. In collaboration with Nusenda Credit Union and UNM, the CFC serves as a valuable resource for the university community. Its mission is to guide students towards financial well-being by offering workshops and personalized consultations focused on fostering healthy money habits. Ultimately, the CFC strives to equip UNM students with the knowledge and confidence to navigate their financial futures successfully, supporting their academic journey and personal growth.
